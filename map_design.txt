Layers:
The numbering of the layers determine the drawing order. The higher the number, the later it is drawn.    
    0. Generated ground layer. Probably use a noise function to generate the ground.
    1. Reserved for later use.
    2. Main layer. This is where the player and most of the objects will be drawn.
    3. Reserved for later use.
    4. Foreground layer. This is where the foreground objects will be drawn.
    5. Reserved for later use.
    6. Highlight layer. On this layer, the player and other entity outlines will be drawn if they are behind a foreground object.
    7. UI layer. This is where the UI will be drawn.
